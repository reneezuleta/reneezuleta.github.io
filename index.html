MIDI Pro was conceived and built by a team of developers and musicians at abirdseyeview.net.

Our goal is to help musicians by providing practice opportunities and composition ideas.

Midi Pro is a starting point for human composers to build upon and add more complex layers of instrumentation.

We experimented with several ideas and implementations and chose to use a mixture of determinism and randomness to construct MIDI sequences that can be tailored to individual preferences,

The generated MIDI sequences can be exported for further enhancing and conversion to other audio formats.

The app allows you to selectively mix percussion and melody tracks so you can play along with your preferred instrument.

The app incorporates:

  • A rhythm section to select the timing and percussion style to use.
    • Several preset rhythms are provided
    • A rhythm editor is provided to modify existing rhythms or to create new ones
    • Several options are provided to control the tempo, measure count, repeats, and wether the melody follows the rhythm beats or uses a random pattern.
  • A percussion section to select percussion elements such as ghost notes and markers.
  • A melody section to select scales, keys, chord progressions and track instruments.
    • Keys, scales and musical instruments can be selected from a list of preset options.
    • Chord progressions can be selected from a list of preset options or can also be constructed by creating custom progressions.
  • An arrange section to group various rhythms into one play sequence.
  • An sound profile section to adjust the percussion velocities and/or to select a different instrument set for the melody play.
